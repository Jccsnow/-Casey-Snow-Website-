About Me

Hello! I'm Casey Snow, an author and occupational therapist. I have been in private practice for 18 years, specializing in sensory integrative techniques to help dissolve anxiety and depression. My work focuses on using the senses to influence the brain and create new pathways, an outside-in method that has proven to be highly effective.

My Books

I have written a series of books called Dis Solve Anxiety and Dis Solve Depression. These books provide practical guidance on using sensory techniques to manage and overcome anxiety and depression.

About Life Balance Inc.

Life Balance Inc. is dedicated to providing sensory profile assessments and personalized therapy sessions. Our approach is designed to help individuals understand their sensory needs and develop effective strategies for managing anxiety and depression.
